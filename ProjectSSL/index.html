About our Club

Our Finance Club of IIT Dharwad started in 2020 aims to bring the students together who are interested to learn and devote their time in the field of finance, club aims to hosting a full schedule of events to help everyone from those with no background in finance to those coming from finance looking to switch focus within the industry.
